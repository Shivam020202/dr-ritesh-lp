Yes. TMS (Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation) is FDA-approved and has been used safely for over a decade. It is non-invasive, drug-free, and does not require sedation. Most patients experience no side effects beyond mild scalp discomfort during the initial sessions.

Many patients experience measurable improvement within hours to days of their first Spravato® session — significantly faster than traditional antidepressants which can take 4–8 weeks. This rapid onset is one of the key advantages for patients with severe or treatment-resistant depression.

Coverage varies by plan. Spravato® (esketamine nasal spray) is FDA-approved and frequently covered by major insurance plans. IV ketamine infusions may require prior authorization or out-of-pocket payment. We provide free insurance verification to clarify your specific benefits before you commit to anything.

TMS sessions are typically 20–30 minutes and require no recovery time — patients often return to work immediately after. Spravato® sessions are approximately 2 hours including a mandatory observation period. Ketamine IV infusions typically last 40–60 minutes in a relaxed clinical setting.

Most adults with a diagnosis of major depressive disorder or treatment-resistant depression are eligible. Dr. Amin will conduct a thorough evaluation during your consultation to determine which treatment is best suited for your specific history, symptoms, and medical background. No prior psychiatrist is required.
